Deliciously Easy Dairy-Free Meals.

Enjoying great food on a dairy-free diet doesn’t need to be difficult! We know that not everyone eats the same way, which is why we created a variety of effortless, dairy-free meals that let you savor all the delicious, satisfying flavors you want—without having to settle.

More Meals, More Choice

It's Dairy Free for All Tastes

We believe dairy free shouldn’t prevent you from enjoying the foods you crave. That’s why we’ve developed dairy-free recipes that span the globe and cater to a wide variety of tastes—so that your dietary preferences never feel like limitations. Explore a host of culinary options: from signature meals like our Baja Pork Bowl and our Sesame-Ginger Chicken & Noodles to seafood fan favorites like our Shrimp & Andouille Paella as well as calorie and carb-conscious meals from our FreshlyFit lineup. Or discover the plant-based goodness from our new Purely Plant meals—all of which are dairy free!

A Fresh Approach

Replace Traditional with Nutritional

Healthy just got stealthy. We take full advantage of gluten- and dairy-free recipes that incorporate whole-food ingredients to make your meals nutritious, delicious, and dairy-free. How do we do it?

  • We swap out traditional dairy and add creaminess from coconut milk and plant-based buttery spreads made from seeds and nuts.
  • We sneak in the veggies: unexpected twists that we add to sauces (like our 8-veggie marinara) and other parts of our meals.
  • We emphasize the good: our meals are loaded with nutrient-dense ingredients, complex carbs, and quality proteins.

    The Secret's in the Sauce

    A good sauce can bring any dish together and turn it into something truly satisfying. It’s also our secret weapon for some of our most popular, better-for-you meals. We replace heavy cream with nutrient-packed powerhouses like squash and cauliflower to give our sauces extra creaminess and thicken things up with nut butters instead of flour. We also use natural sweeteners like honey and coconut sap rather than adding artificial sources like corn syrup.
