Carb-Smart Meals Made 3-Minute Easy

Low-carb recipe this, low-carb meal plan that—​​watching your carbs can feel like you’re riding a rollercoaster. FreshlyFit puts an end to the ups and downs of “all or nothing” crash diets with a more sustainable approach that makes balancing carbs and enjoying great food easier than ever.

Make Your Carbs Count

Make Room for the Good

Counting your carbs? We think you should make every carb count. That’s why we created FreshlyFit—carb- and calorie-conscious meals that help replace empty carbs and excess sugars with complex carbs that work hard. So what does “carb-conscious” mean to us?

  • Thoughtfully designed, chef-crafted, nutritionist-approved meals featuring 20g of carbs or less
  • Whole-food ingredients that form the foundation of flavor-rich meals
  • Nutrient-dense dishes with essential dietary fiber that helps you feel satisfied longer

    • Savor Something Better

    Tired of all the sugar highs and resulting carb crashes? So were we. That’s why we created nourishing meals you can keep in the fridge and enjoy easily on your schedule—so you can avoid the foods that bring you down and get more of the foods that help you push ahead.

  • Hardworking, quality carbs in every meal
  • Dishes designed to nourish your lifestyle
  • Meals that help you avoid the midday carb crashes that slow you down

    • Healthy Just Got Stealthy

    Nutritious that’s Full-On Delicious

    We swap out high-carb ingredients throughout our meals and replace them with something better, like veggie- and protein-forward alternatives that make delicious surprisingly nutritious. How do we make carb-conscious meals so remarkably easy?

  • Zucchini noodles (“zoodles”) in place of traditional carb-heavy pasta
  • Gluten-free almond flour instead of breadcrumbs—the secret charm to our chicken parm
  • Masterful mash with carb-smart cauliflower and greek yogurt

    • Need an easy way to find low-carb meals? Simply sort by carbs online or with the Freshly App and discover a variety of carb-conscious meals with only a click or a tap.
